Marking Scheme Class 10 Chapterwise (Maths)

The Central Board of Secondary Education conducts the CBSE Class 10 board exam in the month of February/March. Every year before the class 10 board examination, CBSE releases the sample paper and marking scheme for the students so that they get well versed with the exam pattern and question paper design. Also, when the board exams gets over, the answers of the class 10 question papers for all the subjects are made available on the board website. The answers of the sample papers and question papers are made available in the form of pdf and are known as Marking Scheme for CBSE Class 10. Many times, the CBSE board website goes down due to which the students find it difficult to download the Marking scheme. So, for their convenience, we have provided the CBSE Class 10 Marking scheme here. Students must go through it to get an idea how the marks are awarded for each step for a particular question.
